# 7. SEO + Content Strategy

To ensure Hair Symmetry captures local traffic in Wexford, PA and the greater Pittsburgh area.

## 1. SEO Optimized Page Titles & Meta Descriptions

**Homepage:**
- **Title**: Hair Symmetry | Premium Hair Salon in Wexford, PA
- **Meta**: Experience Wexford\'s premier salon. From precision haircuts to vibrant balayage, wigs, and nail services, find your balance at Hair Symmetry. Book today!

**Services (Example: Coloring):**
- **Title**: Professional Hair Coloring & Balayage in Wexford | Hair Symmetry
- **Meta**: Transform your look with our master colorists in Wexford, PA. Specializing in balayage, highlights, and color correction. Healthy, beautiful results.

**Stylist (Example):**
- **Title**: Sarah - Master Colorist & Stylist in Wexford | Hair Symmetry
- **Meta**: Book an appointment with Sarah at Hair Symmetry. Specializing in lived-in color, extensions, and modern styling for a flawless look.

## 2. Header Tag Strategy (H1-H4)
- **H1**: Semantic page title/main keyword focus. ALWAYS ONE per page. (e.g., `<h1>Premium Hair Salon in Wexford</h1>`)
- **H2**: Core topic break-downs. (e.g., `<h2>Our Specialty Services</h2>`, `<h2>Olaplex & Eco-Friendly Products</h2>`)
- **H3**: Service names or stylist names within grids. (e.g., `<h3>Balayage & Highlights</h3>`)
- **H4**: Micro-headers or footer groupings.

## 3. Local SEO Content Blocks
Every page (either in the footer or a dedicated pre-footer block) must include **NAP** (Name, Address, Phone) data exactly matching their Google Business Profile.
- **Text**: \"Hair Symmetry is conveniently located at 2578 Brandt School Rd, Wexford, PA 15090, serving clients throughout the North Hills and Pittsburgh area.\"
- **Map Embed**: Verified Google API Map embed on the Contact and Homepage.
- **Schema**: `LocalBusiness` schema targeting `BeautySalon`.

## 4. FAQ Schema Content
Implement valid FAQ structured data on Service pages to claim \"People Also Ask\" space on Google.
- *Examples for Hair Color page:*
  - \"How long does a balayage appointment take?\"
  - \"What is the difference between partial and full highlights?\"
  - \"Do you use Olaplex in your color processing?\"

## 5. Internal Linking Architecture
- Homepage links to top 3 services.
- Every Service page links to the relevant Stylist pages (Stylist Matchmaker).
- Every Stylist page links back to the Services they specialize in.
- Services and Stylists link to recommended Products.
- Products link back to the Services they maintain.

## 6. Content Keyword Targets
- Primary: *Hair salon Wexford PA, Haircut Wexford, Beauty salon near Pittsburgh*
- Secondary/Service specific: *Balayage Wexford, Lash lift and tint, Nail salon Brandt School Rd, Wig consultation PA, Olaplex salon Pittsburgh*
