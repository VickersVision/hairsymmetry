# 4. Stylist Landing Pages

Converting the basic \"Team\" section into high-converting individual landing pages for each stylist. Selling the *person* is as important as selling the *service*.

## Page Structure

### 1. Stylist Hero
- **Visual**: Split screen. Left: High-quality, warm, approachable lifestyle headshot of the stylist. Right: Brief bio and booking button.
- **H1**: Meet [First Name], [Title] (e.g., \"Meet Sarah, Master Colorist\")
- **Bio Copy Tone**: Professional yet personal. Highlighting passion, years of experience, and their specific approach to making clients feel comfortable.
- **Micro-Stats**: 
  - *Experience*: 8 Years
  - *Specialties*: Lived-in Color, Balayage, Hand-tied Extensions
- **Primary CTA**: Book with [First Name]

### 2. The Portfolio (Instagram Integration)
- **Headline**: [First Name]\'s Canvas
- **Visual**: A masonry grid of 6-9 of their best recent works. Ideal if synced dynamically with an Instagram hashtag (e.g., `#HairSymmetrySarah`) or manually curated CMS gallery.
- **UX**: Image click opens a lightbox showing the \"Before/After\" and the specific techniques used.

### 3. Client Love (Social Proof)
- **Headline**: What [First Name]\'s Clients Say
- **Content**: 3-4 highly specific reviews pulled from Google or Yelp that mention the stylist by name.
- **Design**: Elegant quote cards with 5-star icons.

### 4. Stylist\'s Top Product Picks
- **Headline**: [First Name]\'s Must-Haves
- **Layout**: A 3-column product carousel.
- **Copy**: \"To maintain your look at home, these are the products I reach for every day.\"
- **Items**: e.g., Olaplex No. 7, Eco-friendly volume spray. Includes \"Shop Now\" micro-buttons.

### 5. Final CTA
- **Headline**: Ready for a change?
- **CTA**: Reserve [First Name]\'s Next Opening
