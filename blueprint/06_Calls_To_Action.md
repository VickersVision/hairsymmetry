# 6. Primary Calls to Action (CTA) Strategy

A successful salon website relies on frictionless conversion paths. 

## 1. Visual Treatment Hierarchy
There should be three distinct tiers of buttons to guide user attention.

- **Tier 1 (Primary - Max Contrast)**:
  - *Style*: Solid background (e.g., deep charcoal or a vibrant brand accent color), white text, subtle drop shadow.
  - *Usage*: ONLY for \"Book Appointment\" and \"Add to Cart\". Do not dilute this style.
- **Tier 2 (Secondary - Medium Contrast)**:
  - *Style*: Outlined button (Ghost button), brand color text and border, transparent background.
  - *Usage*: \"Meet the Stylists\", \"Shop Retails\", \"View Services\".
- **Tier 3 (Tertiary - Low Contrast)**:
  - *Style*: Text link with an arrow icon (`Learn More ->`) and an underline on hover.
  - *Usage*: Reading blog posts, jumping to detailed policies.

## 2. Placement Logic for \"Book Appointment\"
- **Global Header**: Top right corner, sticky on scroll. Always present.
- **Hero Section**: Front and center above the fold.
- **Services Pages**: Mid-page (after pricing) and at the absolute bottom of the page.
- **Stylist Pages**: Directly under the bio and at the bottom of the page.
- **Footer**: A massive, full-width \"ready to book?\" banner right above the dark footer.

## 3. CTA Copy Variants
Instead of using \"Submit\" or \"Click Here\", use high-intent verb-first copy.

**Booking:**
- `Book Your Experience` (Hero)
- `Reserve Your Spot` (Stylist Pages)
- `Schedule a Consultation` (Wigs/Color Correction)
- `View Openings` (High urgency)

**Shopping:**
- `Shop Our Favorites` (Homepage)
- `Add to Routine` (Product Detail cross-sells)
- `Restock Now` (Email/Retargeting)

## 4. Mobile CTA Optimization
- **The Sticky Bottom Bar**: On mobile, the top sticky header is often too small to tap comfortably. Implement a sticky footer bar fixed to the bottom of the viewport on mobile devices containing a full-width Tier 1 \"Book Appointment\" button. This keeps the conversion point directly under the user\'s thumb at all times.
