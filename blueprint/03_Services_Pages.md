# 3. Services Pages — Modular Landing Templates

Instead of a single, long list of services, Hair Symmetry will use a **Modular Service Template**. This template is designed to rank for local SEO keywords (e.g., \"Hair coloring Wexford PA\", \"Wig consultation Pittsburgh\").

## The Universal Service Page Template

### 1. Service Hero
- **Background**: High-resolution, emotional-resonance image specific to the service (e.g., flowing colored hair for the Color page).
- **H1**: [Service Name] in Wexford, PA
- **Subtitle**: [1-2 sentences on the emotional and aesthetic benefit. e.g., \"Vibrant, dimension-rich color tailored perfectly to your skin tone and lifestyle.\"]
- **Primary CTA**: Book [Service Name]

### 2. Service Definition & Pricing Menu
- **Layout**: Split layout. Left side: Concept/Philosophy. Right side: Menu with pricing.
- **Content Outline**:
  - **What it is**: Detailed explanation of the techniques used.
  - **The Process**: What the client should expect.
  - **Pricing Table**:
    - *Tier 1 / Base Service* .............. Starts at $[X]
    - *Tier 2 / Advanced* .................... Starts at $[X]
    - *Add-ons (e.g., Olaplex treatment)* .... +$[X]
  - **Microcopy**: *\"Pricing varies by stylist level and hair length/density. Exact quotes provided before service begins.\"*

### 3. Before/After Interactive Gallery
- **Component**: A draggable \"Before/After\" slider or a beautiful masonry masonry grid showing real client transformations.
- **Benefit**: Visual proof of competence. Keep images standardized (same lighting/background).

### 4. Preparation & What to Expect
- **Component**: Accordion / FAQ Style.
- **Topics**:
  - *How to prepare for your appointment?* (e.g., \"Come with unwashed hair for color services.\")
  - *How long will it take?* (e.g., \"Block out 2-3 hours for a full balayage.\")
  - *Maintenance requirements.*

### 5. Stylist Matchmaker
- **Headline**: Meet Our [Service] Specialists
- **Grid**: Shows only the 2-3 stylists who specialize in this specific service, with direct booking links to their chairs.

### 6. Service-Specific Testimonials
- **Visual**: Quote block carousel.
- *\"I\'ve never trusted anyone with my color until I came to Hair Symmetry. They fixed a bad dye job and gave me the perfect blonde!\" – Katie M.*

### 7. Bottom CTA & SEO Considerations
- **Closing Headline**: Ready to transform your look?
- **CTA**: Schedule Your Consultation
- **Local SEO Schema Markup Requirement**:
  - Implement `Service` and `LocalBusiness` JSON-LD schema on each page.
  - Map specific `Offers` with `priceSpecification` within the schema.

## Pages to Implement Using This Template:

1. **Haircuts & Styling**: Focus on precision, face framing, and styling longevity.
2. **Hair Coloring**: Focus on balayage, highlights, vivids, and Olaplex health-preservation.
3. **Wigs + Consultation**: *Crucial niche.* Emphasize privacy, empathy, custom fitting, and medical-related hair loss solutions.
4. **Nails**: Focus on sanitation, nail art capabilities, and relaxation.
5. **Lashes & Brows**: Focus on mapping, lifting, tinting, and eye safety.
6. **Waxing**: Focus on comfort, sensitive skin products, and speed.
7. **Men’s Grooming**: Focus on sharp fades, beard care, and efficient service.
